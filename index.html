Listen

Our first album Squnction is out now! We’re an indie rock band based in Boulder, Colorado, playing shows across the Front Range—Boulder, Denver, and beyond. From indie rock tracks like Fifty Five Degrees and Save Your Words to the Latin samba El Zopenco, plus bluegrass and squankin’ funk jams on If You Want and the title track—produced and recorded at The Moat Studio in Boulder. Catch us live for new, unreleased songs.

About Us

We are Squash, an indie-jam band from Boulder, CO. Our music blends classic improvisational rock with modern indie-rock songwriting. Founded by two brothers in 2022, our shows around Boulder and Denver bring a playful stage presence with tightly arranged musicianship. No two live sets are the same—come see why we’re one of the most fun bands in Boulder.
