Welcome to Squash

Listen

Our first album "Squnction" is out now! Streaming on all platforms, this album ranges through the genre spectrum. From indie rock inspired songs like Fifty Five Degrees and Save Your Words, to the latin samba El Zopenco, among bluegrass and squankin' funk jams featured on If You Want and the title track. This debut record was produced, recorded, mixed and mastered by Mason Walker at The Moat studio in Boulder Colorado. We had loads of fun creating this album, and cannot wait to share even more of our music in the future. In the mean time, check us out live to hear these songs, as well as plenty of new music not yet recorded!

Spotify Apple Music YouTube

About Us

We are Squash, an indie-jam band from Boulder, CO. Our music blends the classic improvisational rock techniques with modern indie-rock songwriting. Created by two brothers in 2022, Squash aims to merge a goofy stage presence with masterfully coordinated musicality. The live shows are never done the same way twice!
